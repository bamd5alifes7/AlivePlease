Privacy Policy

Alive Please is a check-in reminder app. This Privacy Policy explains how Alive Please handles information entered or generated in the app.

Information We Handle

Alive Please may store the following information on your device:

  • Your display name
  • The trusted contact email address you provide
  • The recipient title or greeting used in notification emails
  • Check-in dates and check-in status
  • Reminder intervals, quiet hours, care reminder settings, and other app preferences
  • Webhook URL and recent execution logs

How We Use Information

This information is used only to provide Alive Please features, including:

  • Recording whether you completed a daily check-in
  • Showing total check-in days and current streak
  • Scheduling local notifications that remind you to check in
  • Sending an email to your trusted contact when you have not checked in for the period you configured
  • Providing test email and execution log features so you can confirm your notification settings

Where Information Is Stored

Check-in data and most settings are primarily stored locally on your device. Alive Please does not upload your local check-in history to our own database.

Email and Webhooks

If you configure a trusted contact email and enable notification features, Alive Please sends the information needed to deliver the notification through an HTTPS webhook. This may include the recipient email address, email subject, and email body.

This information is used only to send the trusted contact notification or a test email requested by you. If you enter your own custom webhook URL, the handling of data by that service is governed by the service or system you choose.

Sharing of Information

Alive Please does not sell your personal information and does not use your information for advertising tracking. When an email notification is sent, the information required to send that email is transmitted to the email delivery service to complete the notification you requested.

Deleting Information

You can delete locally stored Alive Please data by using Android system settings to clear the app's storage or app data. You can also edit or remove your trusted contact email, webhook URL, and related settings inside the app.

Permissions

Alive Please may use the following Android permissions:

  • Notifications: to show check-in reminders, care reminders, and trusted contact notification status.
  • Receive boot completed: to restore reminder schedules after your device restarts.
  • Internet: to send trusted contact notifications or test emails through a webhook.

Important Notice

Alive Please is not a medical, emergency, rescue, security, or location tracking service. If you or someone near you is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services or a trusted person immediately.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features change or when required by law. The updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the developer contact information provided on the Google Play Store listing.

隱私權政策

Alive Please 是一個報平安提醒 App。本隱私權政策說明 Alive Please 如何處理使用者在 App 中輸入或產生的資料。

我們處理的資料

Alive Please 可能會在您的裝置上保存以下資料：

  • 您的顯示名稱
  • 您指定的親友 Email
  • 收件人稱呼
  • 打卡日期與打卡狀態
  • 提醒間隔、夜間安靜時段、關懷提醒等設定
  • Webhook URL 與近期執行紀錄

資料用途

這些資料只用於提供 Alive Please 的核心功能，包括：

  • 記錄您是否完成每日報平安
  • 顯示累積打卡天數與連續打卡天數
  • 安排本機通知提醒您回來打卡
  • 在您太久沒有打卡時，依照您的設定寄送 Email 通知指定親友
  • 提供測試 Email 與執行紀錄，協助您確認通知設定是否正常

資料儲存位置

Alive Please 的打卡資料與多數設定主要儲存在您的裝置本機。Alive Please 不會將本機打卡資料上傳到我們的資料庫。

Email 與 Webhook

如果您設定親友 Email 並啟用通知功能，Alive Please 會透過 HTTPS Webhook 傳送通知所需資料，例如收件人 Email、信件主旨與信件內容。 這些資料只會用於寄送您設定的親友通知或測試 Email。

如果您自行填入自訂 Webhook URL，該服務如何處理資料將由您指定的服務或系統負責。

資料分享

Alive Please 不會販售您的個人資料，也不會將您的資料用於廣告追蹤。 在寄送親友通知時，通知所需資料會傳送至 Email 寄送服務，以完成您要求的通知功能。

資料刪除

您可以透過 Android 系統設定中的「清除儲存空間」或「清除 App 資料」刪除 Alive Please 保存在裝置本機的資料。 您也可以在 App 內修改或清空親友 Email、Webhook URL 與相關設定。

權限使用

Alive Please 可能使用以下 Android 權限：

  • 通知權限：用於顯示打卡提醒、關懷提醒與親友通知狀態。
  • 開機完成權限：用於裝置重新啟動後恢復提醒排程。
  • 網路權限：用於透過 Webhook 寄送親友通知或測試 Email。

重要聲明

Alive Please 不是醫療、急救、保全、定位或緊急救援服務。若您或身邊的人正處於立即危險，請立刻聯絡當地緊急服務或可信任的人。

政策更新

我們可能會因功能調整或法規要求更新本隱私權政策。更新後的版本會公布於本頁面。

聯絡我們
